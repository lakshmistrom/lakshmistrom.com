Zen Pet History: About Us

Originally established in 1973, Zen Pets was a pioneer in natural, balanced, healthy dog and cat food, as well as supplies. We've taken the time to learn about this unique industry in an attempt to cater to the many questions we receive about our products. Zen Pets continues to be a top-ranked naturalpet food and supply store in Portland.

Zen Pets was the brainchild of Katherine Catsnip, and her long-time business associate, Drake Mallard. Both, with a passion for quality products and customer service, continue to run the business on a daily basis. If you would like to chat with either Kathy or Drake, please come by our store and ask for them. They always have time for questions, comments or concerns.

Thank you for considering Zen Pets Sellwood.
