Before visiting my portfolio, let me tell you a bit about myself.

I am an artist at the core. On top of that, I have alternating layers of graphic design and web development. As a final layer (supported by those below), I am a user interface designer. I believe that my combined artistic and technical background puts me in a good position to design for both form and function. I can design with development in mind and develop to support a good design.

I focus on achieving a balance between form and function. I am not satisfied unless the project looks good and works as it should.

My skills and interests venture into many diciplines, but I focus on Web Development and User Interface Design. Take a look at my portfolio to see some samples of my work and the contact page for professional references and more details of my education.
